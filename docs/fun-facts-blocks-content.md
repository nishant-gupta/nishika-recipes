| Fact Cards | |
|---|---|
| Bees Can Dance | Honeybees communicate by dancing! They do a "waggle dance" to tell other bees exactly where flowers are — including the direction and distance from the hive. |
| Sharks Are Ancient | Sharks have been around for over 400 million years — that's older than dinosaurs, trees, and even most mountain ranges! |
| Bananas Are Berries | Botanically, bananas are true berries — but strawberries are not! A berry must develop from a single flower with one ovary. Strawberries grow from a flower with many ovaries. |

| A Day on Venus Is Longer Than a Year | Venus rotates so slowly that one full day (sunrise to sunrise) takes longer than it takes Venus to orbit the Sun. A Venusian day is 243 Earth days! |

| Jupiter's Day Is Only 10 Hours | Despite being the largest planet in our solar system, Jupiter spins incredibly fast. One full day on Jupiter is just about 10 hours long. |
| Humans Share 99.9% DNA | Every human on Earth shares about 99.9% of their DNA. All our differences — height, eye colour, personality — come from just 0.1% of our genetic code. |

## HOW TO USE IN GOOGLE DOCS

1. Open your Google Doc for the **fun-facts** page
2. Place your cursor where you want the block
3. Insert → Table → choose the right number of columns (2)
4. Copy the rows above into the table
5. The first row header (e.g. "Fact Cards", "Quiz") tells AEM which block to use

For **multiple quiz blocks**, add each quiz as a separate table on the page.
